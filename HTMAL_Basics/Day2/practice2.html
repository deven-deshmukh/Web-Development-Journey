Ordered (numbered) List of Steps for tea id = "tea-section"

  1. Boil water in a kettle
  2. Place a teabag in a mug
  3. Pour the hot water over the teabag
  4. Steep for 3-5 minutes
  5. Add sugar or milk to teste
  6. remove teabag and enjoy
